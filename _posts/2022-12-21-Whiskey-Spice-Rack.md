---
title: "Whiskey Spice Rack"
excerpt: "Great cold-weather cocktail."
categories:
tags:
    - Cocktails
---

* Rye whiskey - 2 oz
* Cardamaro - 1 oz
* Maple syrup - 1/2 oz
* Orange bitters - 2-3 dashes
* Rosemary sprig for garnish

Shake with ice; strain; serve up with rosemary sprig
