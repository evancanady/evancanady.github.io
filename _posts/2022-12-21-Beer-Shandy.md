---
title: "Beer Shandy"
excerpt: "Perfect summer beer cocktail"
categories:
tags:
    - Cocktails
---

* Fresh lemon juice - 1 1/2 oz
* 2:1 simple syrup - 3/4 oz
* Water - 4 oz
* Cold lager, pilsner, or wheat beer - 1

Mix lemon, simple syrup, and water in a pint glass; pour beer over top
