* Oleo Saccharum from 8 lemons (see recipe below)
* Juice of 8 lemons
* Bourbon or Rye Whiskey - 750 ml
* Cognac - 750 ml
* Dark Jamaican rum - 750 ml
* Sparkling wine - 3 bottles

Fill a large punch bowl with ice; add the whiskey, cognac, rum, lemon juice, and oleo saccharum; add bubbly and stir together.

*[Oleo Saccharum](https://evancanady.com/Oleo-Saccharum/) recipe*
