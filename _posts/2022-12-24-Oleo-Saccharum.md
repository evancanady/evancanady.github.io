---
title: "Oleo Saccharum"
excerpt: "The Morgenthaler-method."
categories:
tags:
    - Cocktails
---

* 12 lemons, peeled
* Superfine sugar - 1 lb

Place lemon peels and sugar in a vaccum-seal bag and seal; let sit at room-temp for 4-8 hours until sugar is completely dissolved.

taken from: [Jeffrey Morgenthaler](https://jeffreymorgenthaler.com)
