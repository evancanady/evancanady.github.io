---
title: "Egg Nog"
excerpt: "Never buy store-bought egg nog again."
categories:
tags:
    - Cocktails
---

* Large Eggs - 2
* Super fine sugar - 3 oz (by vol)
* Brandy - 2 oz
* Spiced rum - 2 oz
* Whole milk 6 oz
* Heavy cream - 4 oz

beat eggs in blender for 1 min

slowly add sugar and bland for another minute

while blender is running add brandy, rum, milk, cream

chill thoroughly

serve in wine glass with nutmeg grated over top

taken from: [Jeffrey Morgenthaler](https://jeffreymorgenthaler.com)
