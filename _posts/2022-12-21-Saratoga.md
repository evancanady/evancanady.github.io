* Rye Whiskey - 1 oz
* Brandy - 1 oz
* Sweet vermouth - 1 oz
* Bitters - 2-3 dashes
* Brandied fruit - 2 tbsp. (see note below)

Add brandy and fruit to a cocktail shaker and shake; strain out fruit; add additional ingredients along with ice; stir to chill; strain; serve in a chilled coupe with orange peel

* See [Brandied Fruit](https://evancanady.com/Brandied-Fruit/) recipe *
