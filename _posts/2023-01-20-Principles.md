### Principles of Life

Others' principles to read and steal.

* [Things I Know to be True](https://eightateeight.wordpress.com/2014/09/20/things-i-know-to-be-true/)
* [All I Really Need to Know](http://theory.caltech.edu/~preskill/all-i-really-need-to-know.pdf)
* [How to Write](http://www.evancanady.com/assets/images/ogilvy-memo.jpg)
* [68 Bits of Unsolicited Advice](https://web.archive.org/web/20200429061529/https://kk.org/thetechnium/68-bits-of-unsolicited-advice/)


Most of these are philosophical. How about the basics?
* Eat well and drink a lot of water.
* Exercise. Every day.
* You should probably get more sleep.
* Read more, and don't feel guilty for abandoning something that isn't interesting you.
* Write more. Test your understanding of subjects and self.
* Stop looking at your phone.
* Do hard things to prove to yourself you can.
